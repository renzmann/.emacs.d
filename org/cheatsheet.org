* Keys
** =C-h R= for ~info-display-manual~
** =C-x C-0= to interactively change text size of a buffer
** Reposition cursor
*** =M-r= cycle
*** =M-0 M-r= top
*** =M-- M-r= bottom
** =C-;= in flyspell mode to correct last misspelling
** =M-?= to use ~xref-find-references~
** =M-.= to use ~xref-find-definitions~
** =C-h-.= to use ~eldoc-doc-buffer~ for viewing documentation
** =C-x C-j= to open dired to the folder current buffer is referencing
** =C-x C-q= to make dired editable for easy renaming of filesq
** =M-s o= new occur buffer with search term and press e to edit directly within the occur buffer
*** =C-u N M-s o= to show N lines above/below search results
** =M-s w= search for word
** =M-e= in isearch switch to minibuffer
** =M-@= ~mark-word~
** =C-x C-o= deletes all but one consecutive blank lines
** =M-$= check spelling
